Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership options?

We offer Bronze ($29.99/month), Silver ($49.99/month), and Gold ($79.99/month) memberships, each with different benefits and access levels.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial so you can experience our facilities and classes before committing to a membership.

Are personal trainers available?

Absolutely. Our certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions, tailored to your specific fitness goals.

What amenities do you offer?

Our main facility offers state-of-the-art equipment, group fitness classes, a pool, sauna, locker rooms with showers, and a nutrition bar.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel.

What is your cancellation policy?

Members can cancel with 30 days' notice. Please contact our membership services for more details.
